# Watch Showcase Videos

Add your watch showcase videos to this folder:

- **watch-showcase.mp4** - Main hero video (recommended)
- **watch-showcase.webm** - WebM format for better browser compatibility

## Video Recommendations:

- **Resolution**: 1080x1080 (square format for circular display)
- **Duration**: 10-20 seconds
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264) or WebM
- **Size**: Keep under 5MB for fast loading
- **Content**: Rotating luxury watch, close-up details, or elegant showcase

## Free Video Sources:

You can get free luxury watch videos from:
- **Pexels**: https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/luxury%20watch/
- **Pixabay**: https://pixabay.com/videos/search/watch/
- **Mixkit**: https://mixkit.co/free-stock-video/

## Current Behavior:

- If video files are not found, it will show the poster image (Speedmaster.png)
- The video will autoplay, loop, and be muted
- Circular frame with gold glow effect and floating animation
